Why I do it

Inspire Others through Media

My story begins when I found that my passion of documenting my travels inspired others to step outside their comfort zone and try something new. The idea that doing something I love impacted others positively was enough for me to decide to pursue videography full time over my financially-stable California engineering career.
I firmly believe that with enough drive and hard work anything is possible. This mindset is why I am now chasing my visions relentlessly and working to showcase the amazing world not only for what it is but also how it could be.
